About me

I'm Danton Corbel, learner-for-life. My kids get to learn this stuff at school, I've always wanted to learn this stuff, so I am teaching myself.

I've been inspired by YouTube bloggers such as SeeJaneDrill and April Wilkerson, and films such as "Hidden Figures", and as such have set out to learn what I can about woodwork and coding, two topics I have wished to learn for a long time but not had the time or resources to do so. I still don't have much time, and resources are low, however with the help of Youtube, Code Academy and Khan Academy, progress is being made, slowly!

It can be frustrating at times, when things are not going correctly, or when I have to start again, however I keep reminding myself that it is not easy teaching onself, and I learn from my mistakes.

This journey keeps me inspired in all other parts of my life: building my family, my health, my fitness, my other interests. They can all be done. Well.
